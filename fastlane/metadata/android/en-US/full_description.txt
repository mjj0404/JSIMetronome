JSI Metronome is a minimalist music practice tool built with precision timing and a clean Material 3 interface.

Features:
- Metronome with 30–250 BPM range and subdivisions (1–9)
- Drift-free timing using AudioTrack for low-latency click playback
- Pendulum and dial visualizations
- Pitch pipe for all 12 chromatic notes
- Chromatic tuner with real-time frequency detection
- Background playback via foreground service
- Light, dark, and system theme modes
- Dynamic Material You colors

No ads. No trackers. No internet permission. Fully open source.
